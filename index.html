Game Features

Philippine Traffic Laws Integration

Key traffic regulations from the LTO Comprehensive Driver’s Education Program are integrated directly into the gameplay. Players encounter rules on right-of-way, speed limits, proper signaling, and more, as they navigate each level.

Real-Life Scenario Challenges

The game includes challenging real-life scenarios where drivers must make quick, safe decisions—such as handling sudden pedestrian crossings, navigating slippery roads, dealing with reckless drivers, and following proper procedures at checkpoints.

Driver Feedback and Performance Tracking

After each level or scenario, players receive detailed feedback on their performance, including a breakdown of actions taken correctly and areas that need improvement, such as reaction time, adherence to speed limits, and proper lane use.

About Us

We are a group of five college students passionate about creating impactful digital experiences. Together, we’re developing Road Safety Rookie, a road safety simulator game designed for young drivers, particularly those in their late teens and early twenties who are new to driving. Our game aims to make learning about traffic safety engaging and memorable. Set in the vibrant and varied landscapes of the Philippines—both urban and suburban—Road Safety Rookie uses 3D graphics to replicate real-life driving environments. To ensure accuracy, we’ve integrated key traffic laws from the LTO Comprehensive Driver’s Education Program, giving players a strong foundation in local road safety practices. With gamification elements like points, rewards, and leaderboards, we’re committed to motivating players to hone their skills and knowledge. The user-friendly interface and intuitive design allow for an easy and enjoyable learning experience. Our focus is on realistic scenarios that challenge players to apply safe driving practices in situations they’re likely to encounter in real life. We’re excited to bring this project to life and help young drivers build the confidence and skills they need to navigate the roads safely.
